Dango Music Player

Dango Music Player (DMP) is a music player (wow!). It's designed to be simple yet powerful, with utility for both casual media collectors, users who use streaming services, and power-users with 20,000+ music files in a local collection. Despite still being very much in development, you can try an early version out right now!

Oh and it's open source on GitHub (and various other gits) under the AGPL 3.0 so you can just build the latest version.

Squishy Picture

Squishy Picture (SQP) is a very simple image format, designed to be a learning tool and showcase of simple JPEG-style compression along with a container format.

SQP is dual-licensed under the Apache 2.0 and MIT licences
